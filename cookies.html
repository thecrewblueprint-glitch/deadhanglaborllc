Cookie Policy

Effective Date: June 15, 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed in your browser by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites function, remember preferences, and — in cases that do not apply here — track user behavior across sessions or sites.

Browser storage (specifically localStorage and sessionStorage) is a related technology that stores data locally in your browser without expiration limits tied to the server. This policy addresses both cookies and browser storage together, since they serve similar functions from a user-transparency standpoint.

2. What This Website Uses

This website uses exactly one item of browser storage:

  • Cookie notice acknowledgement (localStorage key: cookieAck) — When you dismiss the cookie notice that appears at the bottom of this site, a localStorage entry is saved in your browser so the notice does not reappear on future visits. This entry contains no personal data — it stores only the fact that you dismissed the notice. It is never transmitted to any server.

No session cookies, persistent identifying cookies, or tracking cookies of any kind are set by this website.

3. What This Website Does Not Use

This website does not use, and has not implemented, any of the following:

  • Analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Plausible, Fathom, Matomo, etc.)
  • Advertising or retargeting cookies (Google Ads, Meta Ads, etc.)
  • Social media tracking pixels (Meta Pixel, TikTok Pixel, X/Twitter Pixel, LinkedIn Insight Tag, etc.)
  • Video embeds (YouTube, Vimeo, or similar) that set third-party cookies
  • Live chat widgets
  • Heatmapping or session recording tools (Hotjar, Microsoft Clarity, FullStory, etc.)
  • Marketing automation or email list platforms
  • A/B testing tools
  • CRM tracking pixels

4. Third-Party Services and Technical Data

Google Fonts

Typography on this website is delivered via Google Fonts. When a page loads, font files are requested from Google's servers. This causes your browser to send a request to Google that may include your IP address and browser information. Google may log this data in accordance with their own policies. Deadhang Labor LLC does not control or receive this data. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Note: Google Fonts does not use cookies or set browser storage on your device. The data exchange occurs at the network request level only.

Formspree (Contact Form)

The contact form on this website submits data to Formspree, a third-party form processing service. Formspree does not set cookies on visitors' browsers through the standard form submission process. However, if you visit Formspree's own infrastructure during submission, standard server logging may occur. See Formspree's Privacy Policy for details.

Web Hosting — Server Logs

Our web hosting provider automatically logs standard server access information including IP addresses, requested pages, browser type, referring pages, and visit timestamps. This data is used by the hosting provider for security monitoring, abuse prevention, and technical operations. It is not linked to personal identity by Deadhang Labor LLC and is not used for marketing purposes.

5. Your Choices

Because this website uses only a single, non-tracking localStorage entry (the cookie notice acknowledgement), there is no cookie consent mechanism to manage beyond dismissing or ignoring the notice.

You can clear the cookie notice acknowledgement at any time by clearing your browser's site data or localStorage for this domain. In most browsers this is done through Settings → Privacy & Security → Site Data. Clearing this entry will cause the cookie notice to reappear on your next visit — no other behavior will be affected.

If you wish to block all browser storage from this site, you can do so through your browser's privacy settings. The site's core functionality — including all content and the contact form — will continue to work normally without any stored data.
