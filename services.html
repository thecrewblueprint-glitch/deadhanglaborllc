What We Offer

Services

Honest scope. Real experience. Long-term contracts preferred.

Staging & Structural Assembly

Ground support builds, structural assembly, and teardown for live events. Hands-on experience with G2, Tait, and PGP Blue Steel ground support systems, Layher scaffolding stages, and large-scale structural configurations for festival and arena environments.

Work performed under qualified direction and according to site-specific safety requirements and manufacturer guidance.

Systems worked: G2 / Tait / PGP Blue Steel / Layher Scaffolding

Load In / Load Out

Full load-in and load-out labor support for arenas, stadiums, outdoor festivals, and corporate events. Experience handling production equipment, staging components, audio gear, video infrastructure, and general event materials in high-tempo, crew-coordinated environments.

Familiar with venue-specific protocols at major Phoenix Metro facilities and travel-based production workflows.

Venues: Mortgage Matchup Center / State Farm Stadium / festival grounds nationwide

Production Support

Labor assistance for audio, video, and lighting crews. Support tasks include cable runs, equipment positioning, case management, video wall panel installation, truss assembly assistance, and general production support as directed.

Comfortable working across departments and adapting to department-specific workflows and communication styles.

Departments supported: Audio / Video / Lighting / General Production

Spotlight Operations

Show-call spotlight support as trained, assigned, and authorized. Experience includes taking cues from the lighting team and maintaining consistent framing and focus through show calls.

Experience: Spotlight support in arena and festival environments

Travel-Based Production Support

Available for festival runs, extended event commitments, and travel-based assignments within the United States when arrangements are agreed in advance. Experience includes out-of-state work in Michigan, Ohio, Virginia, Florida, and California.

Travel note: U.S. assignments by advance agreement

Corporate & Private Event Support

Production labor support for corporate events, private shows, and branded experiences. Familiar with the pace and professionalism standards expected in corporate production environments. Minimum engagement is one week. Week-long corporate contracts are considered when the work is local or I'm already in the area — travel-based corporate work follows the same advance arrangement requirements as all other out-of-state assignments.

Minimum 1-week engagement — local or regional work preferred
