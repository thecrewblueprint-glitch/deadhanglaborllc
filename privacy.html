Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 15, 2026

1. Who We Are

Deadhang Labor LLC ("Deadhang Labor," "we," "us," or "our") is an independent live event production labor contractor based in Phoenix, Arizona. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you visit this website (deadhanglaborllc.com) or submit a contact inquiry through it.

By using this website, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information you voluntarily provide by submitting the contact form on this website. That information may include:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Company or organization name (if provided)
  • Event type (festival, touring, arena/stadium, corporate, or other)
  • Date range of the engagement you are inquiring about
  • Message content — details about your production inquiry

We do not collect payment card information, government identification, or sensitive personal data through this website. We do not use passive data collection tools — such as analytics platforms, advertising pixels, or session recording software — that collect information without your direct input. If that changes, this policy will be updated before any such tools are activated.

3. How We Use Your Information

Information you submit through the contact form is used solely to:

  • Respond to your production inquiry
  • Communicate about potential engagement or contract arrangements
  • Follow up on or close out an inquiry you initiated

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your information with third parties for marketing, advertising, or any commercial purpose unrelated to your inquiry. We do not add you to any mailing list without your explicit consent.

4. How Your Data Is Transmitted — Formspree

Contact form submissions on this website are processed through Formspree, Inc., a third-party form backend service. When you submit the contact form, your submission is transmitted to Formspree's servers and delivered to our business email address. Formspree may temporarily store form submissions as part of its delivery infrastructure.

Formspree's handling of your data — including their data retention and security practices — is governed by their own Privacy Policy, available at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy. Formspree is the data processor for this submission step; Deadhang Labor LLC is the data controller.

After delivery, your inquiry is received and handled directly by Deadhang Labor LLC at our business email address.

5. Other Third-Party Services

This website uses the following additional third-party services that may receive limited technical data as part of normal web operations:

  • Google Fonts — Web typography is delivered via Google's font servers. When a page on this site loads, a request is made to Google's servers to retrieve font files. Google may log the requesting IP address and browser information as part of this process. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy, available at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Web Hosting Provider — Our hosting provider automatically logs standard server access data including IP addresses, pages visited, browser type, and visit timestamps. This data is used for security monitoring and technical operations only and is not linked to personal identity by us.

6. Cookies and Browser Storage

This website uses only minimal browser storage for site functionality. No tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or session-identifying cookies are set by this website. See our Cookie Policy for a complete breakdown of what is and is not used.

7. Data Security

This website is served over HTTPS (TLS encryption). Contact form submissions are transmitted via Formspree's secure infrastructure. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of electronic transmission or internet storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of any information transmitted to or through this website.

8. Data Retention

Contact form submissions delivered to our email are retained as long as they are relevant to an active or potential business inquiry or ongoing communication. Submissions that do not progress to an engagement are deleted when they are no longer needed for business purposes. Formspree's retention of form submission data prior to delivery is governed by their own policy.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of personal information we hold about you from a contact form submission
  • Correction — request that inaccurate information be corrected
  • Deletion — request deletion of personal information we hold about you

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at deadhanglaborllc@gmail.com with the subject line "Privacy Request." We will respond within 30 days. Note that deletion requests apply only to data held directly by Deadhang Labor LLC. For data held by Formspree as part of their form infrastructure, requests must be directed to Formspree.

10. Children's Privacy

This website is intended for business-to-business use by production industry professionals. It is not directed at individuals under 18 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has submitted information through this site, contact us at deadhanglaborllc@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or the services we use. When changes are made, the Effective Date at the top of this page will be updated. For material changes — such as adding new data collection tools or sharing data with new third parties — we will make reasonable efforts to provide notice. Continued use of this website after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of the State of Arizona, without regard to conflict of law principles. Any disputes arising under this policy shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the state and federal courts located in Maricopa County, Arizona.
